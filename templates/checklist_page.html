Emergency Response

What To Do After a Data Breach

If your account was compromised, this checklist helps you secure your digital life quickly and systematically. Follow the priority timeline: critical steps first, then ongoing improvements.

Start Critical Steps Read Full Article

Severity Levels

  • Now: Complete in the next 30 minutes
  • Today: Complete before end of day
  • This Week: Complete for long-term protection

Critical Steps (Next 30 Minutes)

Today

  • Review account recovery methods and update phone/email
  • Remove unknown devices from account activity
  • Verify payment methods and linked accounts

This Week

  • Audit all saved passwords in your browser
  • Replace weak passwords with unique, strong ones
  • Move credentials into a password manager vault

Family Safety

  • Share critical access safely using vault features, not text/email
  • Set up emergency contact permissions
  • Document account recovery methods for trusted contacts

Digital Legacy Planning

Beyond immediate breach response, consider setting up emergency access for trusted beneficiaries. Keep backup codes and recovery methods in a secure, accessible location.
